* The MLC has been granted List A status by the International Cricket Council (ICC), making it a legitimate T20 league and allowing tournament playing records to be used to calculate official format statistics.
* The tournament director, Justin Geale, expressed excitement and gratitude towards the ICC for recognizing the standard and quality of players participating in the tournament.
* The upcoming season of MLC is significant, with the opening game featuring reigning champions MI New York against Seattle Orcas on July 5.
* The tournament has been extended due to demand, with an extra six league matches and a four-game playoff series to be hosted in Grand Prairie, Texas.
* Other notable matches in the opening weekend include Texas Super Kings vs. LA Knight Riders on June 5, Washington Freedom vs. MI New York on July 6, and San Francisco Unicorns vs. LA Knight Riders on July 7.
