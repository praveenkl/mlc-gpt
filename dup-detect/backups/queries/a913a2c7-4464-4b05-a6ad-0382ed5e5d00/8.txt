* The Texas Super Kings (TSK) won their final league match against the Seattle Orcas (SEO) by 37 runs, securing a spot in the playoffs.
* Mitchell Santner played an exceptional shot, hitting a massive six off Ali Sheikh's delivery, showcasing his batting skills.
* Despite Santner's dismissal, TSK posted a competitive score of 177/8 in 20 overs, thanks to Calvin Savage's unbeaten 45 off 27 balls.
* SEO struggled in response, losing early wickets and eventually scoring only 140/9 in 20 overs, with Keemo Paul's 25 off 17 balls being the highest score.
* With this win, TSK advanced to the playoffs in third place and will face MI New York in the eliminator at their home ground in Dallas.
