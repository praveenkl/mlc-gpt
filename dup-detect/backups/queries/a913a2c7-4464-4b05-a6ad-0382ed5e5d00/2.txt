In the 2024 Major League Cricket season, the Texas Super Kings secured their first victory by defeating the San Francisco Unicorns by 7 wickets at Church Street Park in Morrisville. The Super Kings chased down a target of 127, thanks to contributions from Faf du Plessis (34 off 17), Devon Conway (32 off 19), and Aaron Hardie (34 off 23). The Unicorns were restricted to 127 due to consistent wickets, with Matt Short scoring 33. Mohammad Mohsin was the standout performer, taking 4 wickets for 13 runs and playing a crucial role in the Super Kings' victory.
