**Tournament Details:**

* The second season of the Major League Cricket will begin on July 5, 2024, and conclude on July 28, 2024.
* Six teams are participating: MI New York, Texas Super Kings, LA Knight Riders, Seattle Orcas, Washington Freedom, and San Francisco Unicorns.

**Fixture Schedule:**

* The tournament will start with defending champions MI New York taking on Seattle Orcas on July 5, followed by Texas Super Kings vs LA Knight Riders on the same day.
* The league stage will feature 20 matches, with each team playing 5 matches.
* The top four teams will qualify for the playoffs, with the Eliminator (3rd vs 4th) on July 24, Qualifier (1st vs 2nd) on July 25, and the Challenger (Eliminator winner vs Qualifier loser) on July 26.
* The Championship Final will take place on July 28.

**Venues:**

* Two venues will be used for the tournament: Church Street Park and Grand Prairie Stadium.
