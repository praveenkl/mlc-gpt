**Tournament Details:**

* The second season of the Major League Cricket will begin on July 5, 2024, and the final will take place on July 28, 2024.
* Six teams are participating in the tournament: MI New York, Texas Super Kings, LA Knight Riders, Seattle Orcas, Washington Freedom, and San Francisco Unicorns.

**Key Fixtures:**

* The tournament will start with Defending champions MI New York taking on Seattle Orcas on July 5, followed by Texas Super Kings taking on LA Knight Riders in Texas.
* The fixture schedule includes 17 matches, with the top four teams qualifying for the playoffs.
* The playoffs will consist of an Eliminator (July 24), a Qualifier (July 25), a Challenger (July 26), and the Championship Final (July 28).

Note: All matches will be played at either Church Street Park or Grand Prairie Stadium.
