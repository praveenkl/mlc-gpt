**Tournament Details:**

* The second edition of Major League Cricket will start on July 5.
* The league round will feature an additional six matches.
* The play-off series will consist of four matches, to be held at Grand Prairie, Texas.

**Opening Matches:**

* The opening match on July 5 will feature defending champions MI New York (featuring Kieron Pollard) against Seattle Orcas.
* Later on July 5, Texas Super Kings (featuring Faf du Plessis) will face LA Knight Riders (featuring Sunil Narine).

**Notable Players:**

* Steve Smith, Travis Head, and Glenn Maxwell will play for Washington Freedom, coached by Ricky Ponting.
