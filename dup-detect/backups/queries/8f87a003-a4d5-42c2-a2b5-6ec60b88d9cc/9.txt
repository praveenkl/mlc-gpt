**Tournament Details**

* The second edition of the Major League Cricket will be played from July 5 to July 28 in the United States.
* The tournament will be hosted at two venues: Grand Prairie Stadium in Grand Prairie, Texas, and Church Street Park in Morrisville, North Carolina.
* The tournament will follow a round-robin and playoffs format, with the top 4 teams in the points table advancing to the playoffs.

**Schedule**

* The first match will be played between defending champions MI New York and Seattle Orcas on July 5 at Church Street Park, Morrisville.
* The final of the tournament will be played on July 28 at Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas.
