**Tournament Details:**

* The second edition of Major League Cricket (MLC) is set to start on July 6, 2024, with the opening match between MI New York and Seattle Orcas at Church Street Park, Morrisville.
* The grand finale will take place on July 29, 2024, at the Grand Prairie Stadium in Texas.

**Broadcasting Rights:**

* Culver Max Entertainment, owner of Sony Sports Network, has acquired the exclusive television rights for MLC in India for one year.

**Participating Teams and Players:**

* Indian cricket franchises Mumbai Indians, Kolkata Knight Riders, and Chennai Super Kings are participating in the league.
* Popular cricketers such as Quinton de Kock, Marcus Stoinis, and Nicholas Pooran are expected to feature in the upcoming edition of the league.
