**Tournament Schedule:** The Major League Cricket tournament will start on July 5 and will run for three weeks.

**First Match:** The first match will feature 2023 champions MI New York against the Seattle Orcas at Morrisville, North Carolina, at 3:30 PM EDT on July 5.

**Other Matches:** The second match on July 5 will be between the Texas Super Kings and the LA Knight Riders at Grand Prairie Stadium, TX, at 7:30 PM CDT.

**Participating Teams:** Six teams are participating in the tournament, including MI New York, Seattle Orcas, Texas Super Kings, LA Knight Riders, Washington Freedom, and San Francisco Unicorns.

**International Stars:** The tournament will feature several international cricket stars, including Glenn Maxwell, Jake Fraser-McGurk, Nicholas Pooran, Imad Wasim, Devon Conway, and Andre Russell.

**Tickets:** Tickets for all matches, including the four playoffs, are available online through the Official Cognizant Major League Cricket website, with general admission tickets starting at $30 per person.
