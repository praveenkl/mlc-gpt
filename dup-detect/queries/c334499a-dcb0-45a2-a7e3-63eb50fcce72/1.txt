**Tournament Details:**

* The second edition of Major League Cricket will start on July 5.
* The league round will feature additional 6 matches.
* The playoffs will consist of 4 matches, to be held at Grand Prairie, Texas.

**Opening Matches:**

* The opening match on July 5 will feature defending champions MI New York (featuring Kieron Pollard) vs. Seattle Orcas.
* Later on July 5, Texas Super Kings (featuring Faf du Plessis) will face LA Knight Riders (featuring Sunil Narine) in Texas.

**Notable Players:**

* The tournament will feature Australia's 2023 World Cup winners Steve Smith, Travis Head, and Glenn Maxwell, who will play for Washington Freedom, coached by Ricky Ponting.
