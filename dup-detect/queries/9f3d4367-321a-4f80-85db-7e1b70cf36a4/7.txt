* The MLC is set to return for its second season in 2024, after its inaugural edition in 2023.
* The tournament will feature 6 teams, with each team playing each other in a round-robin home and away format.
* The top 4 teams will qualify for the playoffs, with the top 2 teams competing in the Qualifier and the winner advancing to the final.
* The third and fourth-placed teams will compete in the Eliminator, with the winner advancing to the Challenger, and the winner of the Challenger advancing to the final.
* Matches will start at different times in India due to the USA's multiple time zones, with most games starting at 6:00 AM, and some starting at 1:00 AM and 12:30 AM.
* The full schedule of MLC 2024 was announced on May 7.
