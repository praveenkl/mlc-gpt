* The Texas Super Kings won their first match of the MLC 2024 season, defeating the San Francisco Unicorns by 7 wickets at Church Street Park in Morrisville.
* The Super Kings bowled first and restricted the Unicorns to 127 in 17.5 overs, thanks to Mohammad Mohsin's 4/13 and Dwayne Bravo's 2/20.
* The Super Kings chased the target clinically, with openers Faf du Plessis (34 off 17) and Devon Conway (32 off 19) setting the tone, and Aaron Hardie (34 off 23) guiding them to the target in 12.5 overs.
* With this win, the Super Kings jumped to the top of the points table with 3 points from 3 games, while the Unicorns slipped to 5th place with 2 points from 2 games.
