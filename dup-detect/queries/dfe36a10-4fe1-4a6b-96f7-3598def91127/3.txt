* The Texas Super Kings defeated the Seattle Orcas by 37 runs in the final league match of the Major League Cricket (MLC) 2024 at Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas.
* Calvin Savage's all-round performance led the Texas Super Kings to victory, with his unbeaten 45 off 27 balls helping his team reach 177/8 in the first innings.
* The Seattle Orcas struggled in their run chase, with Quinton de Kock and Shehan Jayasuriya providing a solid start, but ultimately falling short, finishing at 140/9.
* Ottneil Bartmann (3/20), Noor Ahmad (2/19), and Calvin Savage (2/23) were the key bowlers for the Texas Super Kings, dismantling the Seattle Orcas' batting lineup.
* The Texas Super Kings finished the league stage with three wins and two losses (two no results), while the Seattle Orcas ended with six losses and a sole victory.
* The Texas Super Kings will play MI New York in the eliminator match.
