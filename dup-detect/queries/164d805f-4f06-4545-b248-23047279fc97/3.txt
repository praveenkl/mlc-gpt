* The 2024 Major League Cricket tournament is set to begin on July 6th at 1:00 AM IST at Church Street Park in Morrisville.
* The opening match will be between MI New York and Seattle Orcas, a rematch of the inaugural edition's final.
* MI New York, the defending champions, have assembled a strong team with multiple overseas players to defend their title.
* Seattle Orcas are seeking revenge for their loss in the 2023 MLC Final and want to start the tournament with a win to boost their rankings in the points table.
