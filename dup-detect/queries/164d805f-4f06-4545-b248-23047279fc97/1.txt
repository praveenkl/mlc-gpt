**Tournament Details:**

* The second edition of MLC will begin on July 6, 2024, with MI New York facing Seattle Orcas at Church Street Park, Morrisville.
* The final will take place on July 29, 2024, at Grand Prairie Stadium, Texas.
* The tournament has gained popularity in India, featuring teams like Mumbai Indians, Kolkata Knight Riders, and Chennai Super Kings.

**Key Players:**

* Quinton de Kock, Marcus Stoinis, and Nicholas Pooran are some of the key players expected to participate.

**Broadcast Team:**

* The broadcast team includes renowned cricket experts like Sunil Gavaskar, Wasim Akram, Danny Morrison, and others.

**Previous Season:**

* In the first season, MI New York won the championship against Seattle Orcas, with Nicholas Pooran being the top scorer and Player of the Season with 388 runs.

**Broadcast Rights:**

* Sony Sports Network has acquired the exclusive television rights for MLC in India for one year, strengthening its cricket broadcasting portfolio.
