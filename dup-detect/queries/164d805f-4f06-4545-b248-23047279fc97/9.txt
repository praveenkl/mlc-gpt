* Accenture has partnered with MI New York, a team in the Major League Cricket (MLC) tournament, as its principal partner for three years.
* The MLC tournament is set to begin its second season this summer, starting July 5th.
* MI New York is a T20 cricket team that plays in the MLC and is part of the MI family, a leading global cricket brand with five T20 teams across three continents and four countries.
* MI New York is the inaugural and defending champion of the MLC, having won the title in 2023.
* The partnership with Accenture will focus on using data analytics, video analysis, and smart technologies to boost the performance of MI New York's coaches and players, as well as improve team operations and fan experiences.
