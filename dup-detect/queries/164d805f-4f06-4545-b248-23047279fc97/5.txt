**Partnership:** Accenture has become the principle partner of MI New York, a team in the Major League Cricket (MLC) tournament.

**Tournament Background:** Major League Cricket began last year and will return next month, featuring franchises with connections to the Indian Premier League (IPL).

**Teams:** The league consists of teams including MI New York, Los Angeles Knight Riders, Texas Super Kings, San Francisco Unicorns, Seattle Orcas, and Washington Freedom.

**Recent Development:** Australian captain Pat Cummins has joined the San Francisco Unicorns, a significant addition to the league.

**MI New York's Performance:** In the 2023 season, MI New York won the championship, defeating Washington Freedom by 16 runs in the eliminator, Texas Super Kings by 6 wickets in the challenger, and Seattle Orcas by 7 wickets in the final.
