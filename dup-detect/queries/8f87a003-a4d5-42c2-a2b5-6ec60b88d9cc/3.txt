**Tournament Details:**

* The second edition of Major League Cricket will start on July 5.
* The league round will feature additional six matches.
* The league round will be followed by a four-match play-off series, to be held at Grand Prairie, Texas.

**Opening Match:**

* The defending champions MI New York, featuring Kieron Pollard, will take on 2023 runner-up Seattle Orcas in the opening match on July 5.

**Other Matches:**

* Faf du Plessis' Texas Super Kings will face off against Sunil Narine's LA Knight Riders in Texas on July 5.

**Notable Players:**

* Steve Smith, Travis Head, and Glenn Maxwell, all Australia's 2023 World Cup winners, will play for Washington Freedom, coached by Ricky Ponting.
